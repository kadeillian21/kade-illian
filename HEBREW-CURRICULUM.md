# Biblical Hebrew Learning Project - REVISED Curriculum

**Purpose:** Daily 30-minute Biblical Hebrew study to achieve reading proficiency in Hebrew Bible narrative texts within 2 years.

**Key Changes from Original:**
- Earlier reading practice (Month 2 instead of Month 3)
- Reduced early vocabulary load (100 words by Month 3 instead of 300)
- Interleaved weak verbs with reading (not 3 months straight)
- Poetry delayed until Month 18 (not Month 13)
- More sustained reading throughout

---

## YEAR 1: FOUNDATION & CORE GRAMMAR (365 sessions)

### MONTH 1: The Alphabet & First Words (30 sessions)

#### Week 1: Consonants (Sessions 1-7)

**Session 1-2:** Letters aleph through yod (א-י)
- Letter names, forms, pronunciation
- Writing practice
- Recognition drills

**Session 3-4:** Letters kaph through ayin (כ-ע)
- Letter names, forms, pronunciation  
- Writing practice
- Review Sessions 1-2

**Session 5-6:** Letters peh through tav (פ-ת)
- Letter names, forms, pronunciation
- Complete alphabet review (all 22)

**Session 7:** Final forms (ך ם ן ף ץ)
- When and why final forms are used
- Recognition practice
- Complete alphabet mastery check

---

#### Week 2: Vowel Points (Sessions 8-14)

**Session 8-9:** Long vowels
- Qamets (ָ = "ah"), tsere (ֵ = "ay"), holem (ֹ = "oh"), shureq (וּ = "oo"), hireq (ִי = "ee")
- How vowels combine with consonants
- Practice reading consonant + vowel

**Session 10-11:** Short vowels
- Patach (ַ = "ah"), segol (ֶ = "eh"), hireq (ִ = "ih"), qibbuts (ֻ = "oo"), qamets hatuf (ָ = "oh")
- Distinguishing long vs short
- More reading practice

**Session 12:** Vocal sheva and silent sheva (ְ)
- Rules for when sheva is pronounced ("uh")
- Practice reading with sheva

**Session 13:** Hateph vowels (ֲ ֱ ֳ)
- Compound sheva sounds
- Guttural letters and hatephs

**Session 14:** Dagesh forte and lene
- Doubling consonants (dagesh forte)
- Hard/soft begadkephat (dagesh lene)
- Reading practice combining everything
- **MILESTONE: Can now decode any Hebrew word!**

#### Week 3-4: First Reading & High-Frequency Words (Sessions 15-30)

**Session 21-23:** Prepositions (בְּ לְ כְ)
- Inseparable prepositions
- How they combine with articles
- Reading: **Genesis 1:1-2** (continuing)
- 10 more vocabulary words

**Session 24-26:** Conjunction (וְ/וּ/וָ)
- The vav conjunction ("and")
- Reading connected phrases
- **Genesis 1:1-3** complete
- 10 more vocabulary words

**Session 27-30:** Consolidation
- Review all grammar so far
- Reading: **Genesis 1:1-5** (full understanding)
- Vocabulary: 30 most common words mastered
